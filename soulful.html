What Is It?

More Than a Cruise.
A Cultural Homecoming.

The Soulful Experience is a series of specially curated river cruise departures designed with the Black travel community at the center. In partnership with AmaWaterways, the world's leading luxury river cruise line, we craft departures that go beyond sightseeing.

Expect culturally immersive excursions, curated programming that connects you to Black history and heritage in each destination, and the rare joy of traveling surrounded by a community that reflects your own story.

From the ancient civilizations of the Nile to the vibrant wine culture of Portugal, every Soulful departure is designed to make you feel not just welcome, but truly celebrated.

Why It Matters

Travel Is a Radical
Act of Self-Reclamation

For generations, Black travelers have been made to feel like guests in luxury spaces, tolerated but rarely truly welcomed. The Soulful Experience exists to change that narrative.

When you sail on a Soulful departure, you are not an afterthought. The programming, the cultural stops, the onboard conversations: all of it is designed with your story at the center. You see yourself reflected in the experiences curated for you.

This is what we mean when we say luxury travel should be culturally affirming. Not just beautiful places, but places where you arrive as your full self.
